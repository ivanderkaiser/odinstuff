Jägermeister Old Fashioned

Jägermeister is basically a German amaro with a great deal of heritage behind it.
Jägermeister is a very versatile liquid to work within the realm of cocktails.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Jägermeister
  • 1 ounce rye whiskey
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 1 dash Dale DeGroff’s pimento bitters
  • Garnish: orange twist

Steps

  1. Add the Jägermeister, rye whiskey, maple syrup and bitters into a chilled Old Fashioned glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.
  2. Express the oils from an orange twist over the glass and drop into the glass.
